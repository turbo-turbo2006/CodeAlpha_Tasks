# 🗄️ CLI Stock Stash: The Offline Portfolio Tracker

## 📉 What is this?
Tired of complicated finance apps asking for your bank login, two-factor authentication, and your first-born child? Welcome to **CLI Stock Stash**. This is a lightweight, terminal-based Python script that simply asks: "What do you own, and how much of it?" 

It saves your stock tickers and share quantities. Every time you fire it up, it remembers exactly what you left there. It's just you, your terminal, and your unshakeable faith in your stock picks.

## ✨ Features
*   **Persistent Local Storage:** Your data is saved right on your machine. No cloud, no data harvesting, no judgment.
*   **Dead Simple Menu:** View your portfolio, add new bags, update quantities.
*   **100% Offline:** Works perfectly without an internet connection. Because sometimes, when the market is crashing, ignorance is bliss.

## 🛠️ Setup & Installation
No external libraries, no bloated dependencies. If your computer can run Python, you're already equipped.
