# 💀 Hangman: The Vocabulary Survival Guide

## 📜 What is this?
Welcome to Hangman! The classic game where you desperately throw letters at the wall to see what sticks, all while a little stick figure slowly meets his inevitable doom. It's educational *and* slightly morbid!

## 🚀 How to Play
1. The computer picks a top-secret word. 
2. You get to guess one letter at a time.
3. Guess correctly, and the letter reveals itself like magic.
4. Guess wrong, and our poor stick-figure friend gets one step closer to the gallows. 
5. You win if you guess the word before the drawing is complete. You lose if... well, you know how it ends.
